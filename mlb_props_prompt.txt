CORE PHILOSOPHY
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Props are matchup bets. Not player reputation bets.
The model doesn't care if the batter is an All-Star.
The model cares how he hits against this pitcher's stuff — at this launch angle — against this hand.

Two data sources. That's it:
  - Fangraphs: K%, BB%, wRC+, ISO, Chase Rate, splits by hand, rolling stats
  - Baseball Savant (Statcast): exit velocity, barrel %, hard hit %, Sweet Spot Rate,
    xBA, xSLG, whiff %, Chase Rate, CSW%, IVB, VAA, Extension, FRV

METRIC GLOSSARY (reference — know these before projecting):
  Chase Rate (batter) — % of out-of-zone pitches swung at. High = easier to K, suppress hits.
  Chase Rate (pitcher) — % of pitches out of zone that batters chase. High = more K ability.
  Sweet Spot Rate  — % of batted balls at launch angle 8-32°. Best BABIP predictor.
  CSW%             — Called Strikes + Whiffs. Best pitch quality proxy per pitch.
  SwStr%           — Swinging strike %. Pure bat-missing rate.
  FRV              — Framing Run Value. Catcher stolen strikes. Adds real Ks to pitcher line.
  IVB              — Induced Vertical Break. Fastball spin-rise. Elite ≥ 18" → hitters swing under.
  VAA              — Vertical Approach Angle. Elite ≥ -4.1° → harder to square up.
  Extension        — Release distance (ft). Elite ≥ 7.4 ft → less reaction time → more Ks.
  BABIP            — Avg on balls in play. Regresses toward .300 over time.

EARLY SEASON PROTOCOL (April – May, fewer than 8 starts / 100 PA):
  - Blend current 2026 stats with 2025 full-season stats
  - Weight: (current PA or starts / threshold) × 2026 + (1 - weight) × 2025
  - Threshold: 100 PA for batters, 8 starts for pitchers
  - Flag with ⚠️ SMALL SAMPLE if blended

PHASE 1 — CONFIRM THE MATCHUP
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Before any projections, confirm:
  - Starting pitcher (confirmed, not projected)
  - Pitcher handedness (L or R)
  - Expected lineup (check beat reporters or official lineup release)
  - Batting order position (leadoff vs cleanup affects PA count)
  - Park (affects HR and hit props — note if extreme park)
  - Weather (pull from RotoWire — wind out = more HR/TB upside)
  - Catcher framing (pull FRV from Baseball Savant):
      Elite framing (FRV ≥ +8): ✅ adds +0.4-0.6 Ks to pitcher line → lean over on K props
      Poor framing (FRV ≤ -8): ⚠️ costs 0.3-0.5 Ks → lean under on K props

Flag any matchup uncertainty:
  [CONFIRMED] — starter locked in, lineup posted
  [PROJECTED] — starter likely but not official
  [UNCERTAIN] — TBD, skip prop or note risk

PHASE 2 — PITCHER PROPS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Props available:
  - Strikeouts (most common — DraftKings, FanDuel, PrizePicks)
  - Innings pitched / outs recorded
  - Hits allowed
  - Earned runs allowed
  - Walks issued

── STRIKEOUT PROJECTION ──────────────────────────────────────

STEP 1: Pull pitcher K rate data from Fangraphs
  Current season (2026):
    - K% overall
    - CSW% (called strikes + whiffs %) from Savant
    - SwStr% (swinging strike %)
    - Whiff % by pitch type (Savant — fastball, breaking, offspeed)
    - Chase Rate induced (% of out-of-zone pitches batters swing at)
        Elite ≥ 33%: strong put-away ability → K projection gets +5% boost
        Poor ≤ 26%: batters lay off → K projection gets -5% trim
    - BABIP allowed (regression signal — see game prompt Phase 5)
    - Number of starts (for early season flag)

  HANDEDNESS SPLITS (from Fangraphs splits tab):
  vs LHB:
    - K%, BB%, ERA, WHIP, Hard Hit % allowed, Barrel % allowed
    - CSW% vs LHB (Savant if available)
    - Chase Rate induced vs LHB (if available)
  vs RHB:
    - K%, BB%, ERA, WHIP, Hard Hit % allowed, Barrel % allowed
    - CSW% vs RHB (Savant if available)
    - Chase Rate induced vs RHB (if available)
  → Use the split matching the opposing lineup's dominant hand
  → Flag if pitcher has ≥5% K% gap by hand: ⚠️ PLATOON SPLIT — adjust projection

  TIER 2 — PITCH QUALITY FLAGS (pull from Baseball Savant, note as flags):
    - IVB (Induced Vertical Break): ≥ 18" on fastball → ✅ K MULTIPLIER
    - VAA (Vertical Approach Angle): ≥ -4.1° → ✅ K MULTIPLIER
    - Extension: ≥ 7.4 ft → ✅ K MULTIPLIER
    - Non-Magnus / Seam-Shifted Wake: note if pitcher known for SSW → extra whiff
    → If pitcher hits 2+ Tier 2 marks: apply +5% to final K projection
    → If all 3 (IVB + VAA + Extension) are elite: apply +8% to final K projection
    → Label: ✅ PITCH QUALITY ELITE

  Prior season (2025) — for early season blend:
    - K% overall, K% vs LHB, K% vs RHB, CSW%, SwStr%, Chase Rate induced

STEP 2: Pull opponent lineup K and chase data from Fangraphs + Savant
  - Team K% vs LHP (if pitcher is LH) or vs RHP (if pitcher is RH)
  - Team Chase Rate vs pitcher hand (if available from Savant)
      High team chase (≥ 30%) vs this pitcher's chase-inducing style = K edge
      Low team chase (≤ 20%) = disciplined lineup, harder to fan
  - Last 7 days team K rate vs pitcher hand
  - Individual batter K% vs pitcher hand for each batter in lineup (1-9)
  - Individual batter Chase Rate (Savant) for key batters — high chaser = more Ks
  - Individual batter career K% vs this pitcher (if 10+ PA)
  - Flag if lineup has 4+ batters with K% >27% vs pitcher hand: ✅ HIGH-K LINEUP
  - Flag if lineup has 4+ batters with K% <18% vs pitcher hand: ⚠️ LOW-K LINEUP
  - Flag if team Chase Rate ≥ 30% vs this pitcher hand: ✅ CHASE-HEAVY LINEUP → K boost

STEP 3: Estimate plate appearances
  Expected PA = Expected IP × (PA per inning estimate)
  PA per inning estimate: ~4.1 (MLB average)
  Expected IP = min(6.0, pitcher historical avg IP per start)
  Short rest cap: 5.0 IP

STEP 4: Project strikeouts
  Base K rate = blended pitcher K% vs opposing lineup's dominant hand
    (use vs LHB split if LH-heavy lineup, vs RHB if RH-heavy, overall if mixed)

  Opponent K rate adjustment:
    Adjusted K% = Base K% vs hand × (opponent team K% vs pitcher hand / MLB avg K% vs hand)
    MLB avg K%: ~22%

  Chase Rate matchup multiplier:
    Pitcher Chase Rate induced ≥ 33% AND team Chase Rate ≥ 30%: × 1.07
    Pitcher Chase Rate induced ≥ 33% AND team Chase Rate neutral (20-29%): × 1.03
    Pitcher Chase Rate induced ≤ 26% AND team Chase Rate ≤ 20%: × 0.93
    Otherwise: no chase adjustment

  Career matchup adjustment:
    If key batters (accounting for 3+ PA each) have career K% vs this pitcher ≥35%: × 1.05
    If key batters have career K% vs this pitcher ≤15%: × 0.95

  Tier 2 pitch quality boost (from Step 1 flags):
    2 elite Tier 2 marks (IVB/VAA/Extension): × 1.05
    All 3 elite: × 1.08
    No elite marks: no adjustment

  Projected K = Adjusted K% × Expected PA

  Early season blend:
    Projected K = (starts/8 × 2026 projection) + ((8-starts)/8 × 2025 projection)
    ⚠️ SMALL SAMPLE flag if blended

STEP 5: Apply game environment adjustments
  Weather (wind in from OF ≥ 15 mph): +0.25-0.5 K (hitters defensive, more Ks)
  Weather (wind out): -0.1 to -0.2 K (hitters aggressive, harder to fan)
  Catcher FRV ≥ +8 (elite framing): +0.4-0.6 K
  Catcher FRV ≤ -8 (poor framing): -0.3-0.5 K
  Catcher FRV -7 to +7: no adjustment
  Extreme pitcher's park: +0.2 K
  Hitter's park: -0.1 K

STEP 6: Compare to Vegas line
  Book line: [X.5 Ks at -120/-115]
  Model projection: [X.X Ks]
  Edge = Model - Line

  Edge thresholds:
    Strong K edge: ≥ 0.75 Ks
    Moderate K edge: 0.5 - 0.74 Ks

Show all steps:
  Pitcher: [Name] | Hand: [L/R] | Expected IP: [X.X]
  K%: [X.X%] (2026) | CSW%: [X.X%] | SwStr%: [X.X%]
  Opponent K% vs [hand]: [X.X%] → Adj K%: [X.X%]
  Expected PA: [X.X] | Projected Ks: [X.X]
  Adjustments: [weather/park/catcher] → Final: [X.X Ks]
  Line: [X.5] | Edge: [+/- X.X]

── INNINGS PITCHED / OUTS RECORDED ──────────────────────────

STEP 1: Pull pitcher historical avg IP per start (current + last season)
STEP 2: Apply adjustments
  Short rest (<4 days): cap at 5.0 IP
  Pitcher coming off long outing (100+ pitches last start): -0.3 IP
  Manager quick hook tendency (pull if ERA climbs early): note
  Bullpen fatigue (top relievers used back-to-back): manager may extend starter

STEP 3: Project outs recorded
  Projected outs = Projected IP × 3

STEP 4: Compare to PrizePicks / book line
  Edge threshold: ≥ 0.5 IP edge

  Projected IP: [X.X] | Projected outs: [XX]
  Line: [X.5 IP / XX.5 outs] | Edge: [+/-]

PHASE 3 — BATTER PROPS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Props available:
  - Hits (most common — PrizePicks, DraftKings)
  - Total bases (TB)
  - Home runs
  - RBI
  - Runs scored
  - Walks
  - Strikeouts (batter)

── BATTER DATA PULL ──────────────────────────────────────────

For each batter prop, pull from Fangraphs:
  Current season (2026):
    - BA, OBP, SLG, wOBA
    - wRC+ overall
    - K%, BB%, ISO
    - BABIP (note if elevated — regression risk)
    - PA count (for early season flag — threshold: 100 PA)

  Prior season (2025) — for early season blend:
    - BA, wRC+, ISO, wRC+ splits by hand

  HANDEDNESS SPLITS (pull from Fangraphs splits tab — always use vs pitcher hand):
  vs LHP:
    - BA, OBP, SLG, wRC+, K%, BB%, ISO, BABIP
  vs RHP:
    - BA, OBP, SLG, wRC+, K%, BB%, ISO, BABIP
  → Always use the split matching the opposing pitcher's hand
  → Flag if platoon split is extreme (>30 wRC+ gap): ⚠️ PLATOON DISADVANTAGE or ✅ PLATOON EDGE
  → Example: batter with .310 BA vs RHP but .220 BA vs LHP facing LHP = fade the over on hits

  BATTER vs PITCHER CAREER HISTORY:
    - Career AB, H, HR, BB, K, OPS vs this specific pitcher
    - Minimum sample: 10 PA to be meaningful
    - If < 10 PA: note as ⚠️ SMALL SAMPLE — use splits instead
    - If 20+ PA: weight career OPS at 25% alongside current season splits
    - Flag if batter is historically dominant (OPS ≥ .950 vs pitcher): ✅ MATCHUP EDGE
    - Flag if pitcher historically dominates batter (OPS ≤ .550, K rate ≥ 35%): ⚠️ AVOID OVER

  From Baseball Savant:
    - Exit velocity avg
    - Hard hit % (95+ mph) overall AND vs pitcher hand if available
    - Barrel %
    - Sweet Spot Rate (launch angle 8-32°):
        ≥ 40% elite: high quality contact → hits and TB over more reliable
        ≤ 32.5% poor: ground ball / pop-up tendency → suppress hit/TB overs
        Note: Hard Hit % without Sweet Spot Rate = "loud outs" trap
    - xBA, xSLG, xwOBA
    - Chase Rate (% of out-of-zone pitches swung at):
        ≥ 30% high chase: batter vulnerable to breaking balls away → suppress hit overs
        ≤ 20% low chase: disciplined eye → more counts, more walks, better OBP
        Use this vs pitcher's chase-inducing tendencies (see pitcher Chase Rate)
    - Whiff % overall AND vs pitcher hand (affects K prop + hits prop)
    - Sprint speed (affects infield hits / SB props)
    - Pull % (affects HR projection vs park RF/LF)

  Rolling stats from Fangraphs:
    - Last 7 days: BA, wRC+, HR, SLG (hot/cold streak flag)
    - Last 14 days: same
    - Last 30 days: same
    → If 7-day BA diverges >50 pts from vs-hand split: apply 30% weight to recent form

── HITS PROJECTION ───────────────────────────────────────────

STEP 1: Base hit rate (always use handedness split)
  Pull batter BA vs pitcher hand (LHP or RHP):
    Current 2026: BA vs hand
    If early season (< 100 PA): blended BA = (PA/100 × 2026 BA vs hand) + ((100-PA)/100 × 2025 BA vs hand)
  This is your starting hit rate — never use overall BA if split is available

STEP 2: Adjust for pitcher quality (use pitcher splits vs batter hand)
  Pitcher K% vs batter hand:
    If K% vs hand > 28%: hit rate × 0.88
    If K% vs hand 23-27%: hit rate × 0.94
    If K% vs hand < 20%: hit rate × 1.04
  Pitcher BABIP allowed vs batter hand (use if available from Savant)
  Pitcher Hard Hit % allowed vs batter hand: if > 40% allowed → slight hit rate bump

  Chase Rate matchup (batter Chase Rate vs pitcher Chase Rate induced):
    Batter Chase ≥ 30% AND pitcher Chase induced ≥ 33%:
      → Batter chases pitcher's best out-pitch → hit rate × 0.92
    Batter Chase ≤ 20% AND pitcher Chase induced ≤ 26%:
      → Disciplined batter vs pitcher who can't put away → more counts → hit rate × 1.05
    Otherwise: no chase adjustment

  Sweet Spot Rate quality check (batter contact profile):
    Batter Sweet Spot Rate ≥ 40% AND Hard Hit % ≥ 42%:
      → True contact quality → BABIP likely stable or above avg → hit rate × 1.04
    Batter Sweet Spot Rate ≤ 32.5% despite Hard Hit % ≥ 40%:
      → Loud outs trap → hits likely below exit velo would suggest → hit rate × 0.96
    Otherwise: neutral

  Career matchup adjustment (batter vs this specific pitcher):
    If 20+ career PA vs pitcher:
      Career BA vs pitcher carries 25% weight
      Final BA = (0.75 × split-adjusted BA) + (0.25 × career BA vs pitcher)
    If 10-19 career PA: note as reference only, no weight adjustment
    If < 10 PA: ignore, use splits only

STEP 3: Estimate plate appearances
  Batting order position → PA estimate:
    Leadoff: ~4.5 PA
    2nd: ~4.3 PA
    3-5 hole: ~4.0-4.1 PA
    6-9: ~3.5-3.8 PA

STEP 4: Project hits
  Base projected hits = Hit rate × Estimated PA
  Early season adjustment: apply blended hit rate

STEP 5: Apply park and weather adjustments
  High BABIP park (Coors, Fenway): +0.05-0.10 hits
  Low BABIP park (Oracle, Petco): -0.05-0.10 hits
  Wind out: +0.03-0.05 (slightly more hard contact)
  Wind in: -0.03-0.05

STEP 6: 7-day form weight
  If last 7-day BA diverges > 50 pts from season avg:
    Final = (0.70 × projected) + (0.30 × 7-day adjusted)
  Else: Final = projected

STEP 7: Compare to line
  PrizePicks line: 0.5 hits (most common) or 1.5 hits
  DraftKings/FanDuel may offer 0.5 to 1.5

  Edge for 0.5 hits: if projected ≥ 0.60 → take over; if ≤ 0.40 → take under
  Edge for 1.5 hits: if projected ≥ 1.65 → take over; if ≤ 1.35 → take under

Show:

  BA vs hand: [X.XX] (2026) | Pitcher K% vs hand: [X.X%]
  Hit rate adj: [X.XX] | Estimated PA: [X.X]
  Projected hits: [X.XX] | 7d adj: [X.XX if applied]
  Park adj: [note] | Final: [X.XX]
  Line: [0.5 or 1.5] | Edge: [+/-]

── TOTAL BASES PROJECTION ────────────────────────────────────

STEP 1: Pull SLG vs pitcher hand (blended if small sample)
STEP 2: Calculate projected TB
  Projected TB = SLG × Estimated PA
  (SLG = (1B×1 + 2B×2 + 3B×3 + HR×4) / AB — already baked in)

STEP 3: Adjust for pitcher power suppression
  Pitcher Barrel % allowed vs hand:
    ≤ 5% barrel allowed: × 0.90 (power suppressor)
    5-8%: neutral
    ≥ 9%: × 1.10 (gives up hard contact)

  Pitcher HR/FB% vs hand:
    ≤ 8%: -0.10 TB
    ≥ 14%: +0.15 TB

  Batter Sweet Spot Rate quality check:
    Sweet Spot ≥ 40% + Hard Hit ≥ 42%: TB projection × 1.05 (true XBH producer)
    Sweet Spot ≤ 32.5% despite high exit velo: TB projection × 0.95 (loud grounders/popups)
    Otherwise: neutral

STEP 4: Apply park HR factor
  High HR park (Coors, Yankees): +0.10-0.15 TB
  Low HR park (Oracle, Petco): -0.10-0.15 TB

STEP 5: Apply weather
  Wind 15+ mph out: +0.15-0.25 TB
  Wind 15+ mph in: -0.15-0.25 TB

STEP 6: Compare to line
  PrizePicks TB line: typically 1.5 TB
  Edge threshold: ≥ 0.20 TB

  Estimated PA: [X.X] | Base TB: [X.XX]
  Pitcher barrel% allowed: [X.X%] → Adj: [X.XX]
  Park adj: [note] | Weather adj: [note]
  Final TB: [X.XX] | Line: [1.5] | Edge: [+/-]

── HOME RUN PROJECTION ───────────────────────────────────────

STEP 1: Pull batter HR rate metrics
  - ISO vs pitcher hand
  - Barrel % (Savant)
  - HR/PA rate (current + 2025 blend if small sample)
  - Pull % (affects park HR factor — pull hitters in favorable parks)

STEP 2: Pull pitcher HR allowed metrics
  - HR/9 vs hand
  - HR/FB% vs hand
  - Barrel % allowed (Savant)
  - Flyball % allowed

STEP 3: Estimate HR probability
  Base HR/PA rate (batter)
  Pitcher HR factor = (pitcher HR/FB% vs hand) / (MLB avg HR/FB% ≈ 11%)
  Adjusted HR/PA = Base HR/PA × Pitcher HR factor
  HR probability = Adjusted HR/PA × Estimated PA

STEP 4: Apply park factor
  Park HR factor (pull from Fangraphs):
    > 110: multiply × 1.10
    90-110: neutral
    < 90: multiply × 0.90

STEP 5: Apply weather
  Wind 15+ mph out toward batter's pull side: × 1.15
  Wind 15+ mph in: × 0.85

STEP 6: Compare to line
  HR prop is usually Over 0.5 at +200 to +350
  Model edge: if HR prob ≥ 20%, Over 0.5 at +350 or better = value
  If HR prob ≤ 10%, Under 0.5 where available

  Pitcher HR/FB% vs hand: [X.X%] → HR factor: [X.XX]
  Adj HR/PA: [X.XXX] | Est PA: [X.X]
  HR probability: [X.X%] | Park factor: [note]
  Weather: [note] | Final HR prob: [X.X%]
  Line: [O0.5 +XXX] | Model value: [YES/NO at these odds]

── RBI PROJECTION ────────────────────────────────────────────

Note: RBI is lineup-dependent. Only project for batters hitting 3-5.

STEP 1: Estimate teammates on base when batter comes up
  Approximate: team OBP × runners ahead × PA
  Simplification: use team wOBA / 1.2 as proxy for base occupancy rate

STEP 2: Project batter's RBI rate
  Base RBI/PA = historical RBI/PA (current season)
  Blend if < 100 PA

STEP 3: Adjust for pitcher quality
  Pitcher K% vs hand > 26%: fewer base runners, fewer RBI chances
  Adjustment: × (1 - ((K% vs hand - 22%) / 100))

STEP 4: Compare to PrizePicks line (usually 0.5 RBI)
  If projected RBI ≥ 0.60 → consider over
  Context-dependent — skip if lineup missing key OBP guys ahead
