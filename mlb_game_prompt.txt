CORE PHILOSOPHY
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

This model does not bet ERA. ERA is noise.
This model bets SIERA, xFIP, wRC+, and exit velocity.
The public prices off last week's box score.
We price off underlying process metrics.
That gap is where the money lives across a 162-game season.

EARLY SEASON PROTOCOL (April – May, fewer than 8 starts):
  - Blend current 2026 stats with 2025 full-season stats
  - Weight: (current starts / 8) × 2026 stat + (1 - current starts / 8) × 2025 stat
  - Flag any projection using blended stats with ⚠️ SMALL SAMPLE
  - As starts accumulate, weight shifts fully to 2026 by June

PHASE 5 — PITCHER MODEL (build projected runs allowed)
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: Blend SIERA and xFIP into composite ERA estimate
  Composite ERA = (0.60 × SIERA) + (0.40 × xFIP)
  Note: SIERA weighted higher — accounts for batted ball mix better

  Early season adjustment (< 8 starts):
  Composite ERA = (starts/8 × 2026 Composite) + ((8-starts)/8 × 2025 Composite)
  Flag with ⚠️ SMALL SAMPLE if blended

STEP 2: Adjust for opponent offense
  Opponent Offensive Adjustment = (opponent wRC+ vs pitcher hand) / 100
  Adjusted ERA = Composite ERA × Opponent Offensive Adjustment

STEP 3: Apply park factor
  Park Adjusted ERA = Adjusted ERA × (park factor / 100)

STEP 4: Apply weather adjustment (from Phase 1)
  Weather ERA adjustment: for every +1 run weather adds to total,
  split evenly between both pitchers

STEP 5: Project innings pitched
  Expected IP = min(6.0, historical avg IP per start)
  For short-rest starters: cap at 5.0 IP

STEP 6: Project runs allowed by starter
  Starter Runs = (Park Adjusted ERA / 9) × Expected IP

STEP 7: Project bullpen runs
  Bullpen IP = 9 - Expected IP (starter)
  Bullpen ERA estimate = pull team bullpen ERA last 7 days
  Bullpen Runs = (Bullpen ERA / 9) × Bullpen IP

STEP 8: Total projected runs allowed
  Projected Runs Allowed = Starter Runs + Bullpen Runs

Show every step. Example:
  Pitcher: [Name]
  SIERA: 3.21 | xFIP: 3.45 → Composite ERA: 3.31
  vs [opponent] wRC+ [vs hand]: 108 → Offensive Adj: 1.08
  Adjusted ERA: 3.57 | Park Factor: 105 → Park Adj ERA: 3.75
  Expected IP: 5.2 | Starter Runs: (3.75/9) × 5.2 = 2.17
  Bullpen ERA (7d): 4.12 | Bullpen IP: 3.8 | Bullpen Runs: 1.74
  Total Projected Runs Allowed: 3.91

Do this for BOTH teams.

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
PHASE 6 — OFFENSE MODEL (build projected runs scored)
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: League baseline
  MLB average runs/game: ~4.5 (adjust if current season avg differs)

STEP 2: Team offensive rating
  Offensive Rating = team wRC+ (vs opposing pitcher hand) / 100
  Base Projected Runs = 4.5 × Offensive Rating

STEP 3: Adjust for opposing pitcher quality
  Pitcher Quality Factor = 1 - ((4.50 - Projected Runs Allowed by starter) / 4.50 × 0.4)
  Adjusted Projected Runs = Base Projected Runs × Pitcher Quality Factor

STEP 4: Apply park factor
  Park Adjusted Runs = Adjusted Projected Runs × (park factor / 100)

STEP 5: Apply weather adjustment (from Phase 1)
  Add weather run adjustment split across both teams

STEP 6: Apply recent form weight (last 7 days)
  If team wRC+ last 7 days diverges >15 pts from season avg:
  Final Runs = (0.70 × Park Adjusted Runs) + (0.30 × 7-day adjusted)
  Else: Final Runs = Park Adjusted Runs

Show all steps for both teams.
